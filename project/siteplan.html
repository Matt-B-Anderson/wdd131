Project Site Plan

Site Name:

Woodland Hills Accounting Needs

I chose this name because this is going to be a site for an accounting company specifically located in Woodland Hills California. A possibe domain could be whan.com

Site Purpose:

This site provides a place where accounting services can be requested in the Woodland Hills area.

Scenarios:

  • Who should I go to for preparing my personal or business taxes?

  • Where is the company located?

  • What services are provided?

  • Who is the owner of the company?

  • Can I schedule an appointment?
