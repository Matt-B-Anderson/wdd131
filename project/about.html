History

Woodland Hills Accounting Needs (WHAN) began as a small home office in 2003 with a single goal — to help local families and small businesses in Woodland Hills navigate their finances with confidence. Founded by a former corporate accountant turned community advocate, WHAN quickly gained a reputation for honesty, transparency, and attention to detail. As the demand for personalized accounting services grew, so did we. By 2008, WHAN had opened its first office space, expanded its service offerings, and welcomed a team of passionate professionals dedicated to simplifying the complexities of finance. Over the years, we’ve embraced new technologies, adapted to ever-changing tax laws, and remained committed to offering the same client-first service that started it all. Today, WHAN proudly serves clients across the region, from independent contractors and start-ups to established businesses seeking long-term financial guidance. Our history is built on trust, growth, and a belief that good accounting should empower—not overwhelm—you.
